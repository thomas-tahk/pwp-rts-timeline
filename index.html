A Brief History of Real Time Strategy Games

What is RTS?

RTS, short for Real Time Strategy is a genre of video games characterized by minute, tight controls in real-time action. RTS games are often described as chess on steroids.

Origins of RTS

RTS has its origins in board games but rise of computing gave it the distinguishing feature of playing in real-time as opposed to turn-based.

The Elite Four of RTS

RTS has been dominated by (at least in audience memory and discussion) a handful of franchises:

  • Warcraft
  • Command & Conquer
  • Age of Empires
  • Starcraft

Legacy & E-Sports

Though the golden years may be past, RTS games' living legacy has birthed new genres, shaped and transformed competitive gaming, and maybe at the cusp of a new renaissance.
