A Brief and Biased History of Real Time Strategy Games

Origins of RTS

Subgenre of video games that diverged from its roots in table-top strategy and war games, shed turn-based mechanics, and established an identity of its own.

What is an RTS?

RTS, short for Real Time Strategy is a genre of video games characterized by simultaneous play, called real-time and often fierce competition. RTS games are often described as chess on steroids.

RTS GOATs - Warcraft

    Warcraft was and still is one of the defining franchises in RTS history throughout the decades. Developed by Blizzard Entertainment
  • Warcraft: Orcs & Humans [1995]
  • Warcraft II [1996]
  • Warcraft III [2002]
  • Warcraft III: Reforged [2020]
  • Warcraft II: Remastered [2024]
  • Warcraft I: Remastered [2024]

RTS GOATs - Command & Conquer

    Command & Conquer may well be the most definitive RTS series in history and is certainly the most prolific. However, we'll narrow our focus to the most critically acclaimed titles developed by Westwood Studios.
  • Command & Conquer [1995]
  • Command & Conquer: Red Alert [1996]
  • Command & Conquer: Sole Survivor [1997]
  • Command & Conquer: Tiberian Sun [1999]
  • Command & Conquer: Red Alert 2 [2000]
  • Command & Conquer: Renegade [2000]

RTS Goats - Age of Empires

    Age of series are considered by many to be pinnacle of RTS, bar none.
  • Age of Empires 1 [1997]
  • Age of Empires 2 [1999]
  • Age of Mythology [2002]
  • Age of Empires 3 [2005]
  • Age of Empires 4 [2021]

Starcraft: Broodwar [1998]

Starcraft and its same-year expansion Broodwar is perhaps the most significant game to ever release for all of RTS, and is easily a contender for the game that stands above all.

Starcraft 2 [2010, 2013, 2015]

Starcraft 2 is probably the most well known and played RTS in the world since its initial release in 2010 as the long awaited successor to Broodwar. It has undoubtedly carved itself an unshakable seat at the table of the greatest RTS games ever made, outside the massive shadow of its predecessor.

eSports

RTS games trailblazed competitive gaming to its pinnacle and sustained popularity to the modern day, including its branding as eSports.

Legacy

Golden years may have passed but RTS endures and has an ongoing legacy that continues to influence games in and out of its genre, gaming culture, and more.
