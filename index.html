A Brief History of Real Time Strategy Games

Origins of RTS

Subgenre of video games that diverged from its roots in table-top strategy and war games, shed turn-based mechanics, and established an identity of its own.

What is an RTS?

RTS, short for Real Time Strategy is a genre of video games characterized by simultaneous play, called real-time and often fierce competition. RTS games are often described as chess on steroids.

The Elite Four of RTS

    RTS has been dominated by (at least in audience memory and discussion) a handful of franchises:
  • Warcraft
  • Command & Conquer
  • Age of Empires
  • Starcraft

Legacy & E-Sports

Though the golden years may be past, RTS games' living legacy has birthed new genres, shaped and transformed competitive gaming, and may be at the cusp of a new renaissance.
