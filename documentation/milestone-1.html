Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose:

This project is a front-end website that aims to inform on and celebrate the history of various games that defined the Real Time Strategy genre using a visual timeline.

Audience:

This site is designed primarily for fans, players both current and former, and anyone else curious enough to learn more about the genre. This site is also serving as a demonstration to potential employers in video game development, publishing, or content creation.

Goal:

The primary goal of this site is to share information and enthusiasm about RTS video games and their history with an opportunity for users to reach out to me to comment/discuss on the content, note corrections, etc. I also hope to use this website as a way to demonstrate some of my abilities in web development to a potential employer.
