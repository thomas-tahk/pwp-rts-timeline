Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose:

This project is a front-end only website that aims to inform and celebrate the history of various games that defined the Real Time Strategy genre using a visual timeline.

Audience:

This site is designed primarily for fans, players both current and former, and anyone else curious enough to learn more about the genre. This site is also serving as a demonstration to potential employers in video game development, publishing, or content creation.

Goal:

The primary goal of this site is to share in nostalgia, enthusiasm, and interesting facts about RTS video games with an opportunity for users to reach out to me to comment on the content, note corrections, etc. Secondarily, this website will serve to demonstrate my abilities in web development and serve as a possible point of contact with a potential employer.

It’s rare to find someone with the right combination of experience, growth oriented mindset, and enthusiasm for the work and product.

User Story

As a user, I want to appreciate and enjoy the content the site’s developer has curated and be able to talk about it to that developer. In the back of my mind, I am also determining whether this developer may be a good fit for my team at work.
